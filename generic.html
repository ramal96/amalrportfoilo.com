Cover page design

Thrilled to have contributed to the cover design for Dr. Paul Kwamina Anfam Mensah's inspiring book, "Breaking Through: The Will and the Ability To Go Forward." Using Adobe InDesign, I created a visually striking design that reflects the book's themes of overcoming obstacles and achieving personal growth. The bold typography and dynamic imagery symbolize the strength and determination that Dr. Mensah embodies. I hope this cover design captures the essence of his fascinating journey and encourages readers to explore his inspiring story.
